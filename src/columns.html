Expanding Columns

As I was thinking of potential website layouts, something like this popped into my head—three columns (rows on mobile) and a navbar that expand on click, populating with content. I didn't really know what I was getting myself into, I just wanted to see if I could make it.

Once it was all said and done, I tested it on mobile and BOY was the performance terrible. So it lives here now for your enjoyment (if your device can smoothly animate it lol).
